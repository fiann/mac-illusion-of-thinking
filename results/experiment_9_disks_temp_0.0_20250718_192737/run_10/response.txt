# Solving the Tower of Hanoi Puzzle with 9 Disks

The Tower of Hanoi puzzle has a well-known recursive solution. For 9 disks, I'll solve it systematically.

## Strategy
1. Move disks 1 through 8 from peg 0 to peg 1 (using peg 2 as auxiliary)
2. Move disk 9 from peg 0 to peg 2
3. Move disks 1 through 8 from peg 1 to peg 2 (using peg 0 as auxiliary)

## Solution Algorithm
I'll use a recursive approach where:
- To move n disks from source to destination using auxiliary:
  - Move n-1 disks from source to auxiliary using destination
  - Move the nth disk from source to destination
  - Move n-1 disks from auxiliary to destination using source
