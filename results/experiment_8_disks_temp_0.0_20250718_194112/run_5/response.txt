# Tower of Hanoi Solution for 8 Disks

I'll solve the Tower of Hanoi puzzle for 8 disks using a recursive approach.

## Understanding the Problem
- We have 8 disks of different sizes (1 through 8, with 1 being the smallest)
- Initial state: All disks are on peg 0, arranged from largest (8) at bottom to smallest (1) at top
- Goal: Move all disks to peg 2
- Rules: Only move one disk at a time, never place a larger disk on a smaller one

## Solution Approach
The Tower of Hanoi has a well-known recursive solution. For n disks:
1. Move n-1 disks from source to auxiliary peg
2. Move the largest disk from source to destination peg
3. Move n-1 disks from auxiliary to destination peg

For 8 disks, we need 2^8 - 1 = 255 moves.
